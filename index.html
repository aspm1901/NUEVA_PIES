Welcome to Bethany's Pie Shop

History

At Bethany's Pie Store, we're more than just a bakery, we're a slice of tradition and passion. Founded by Bethany Williams, our story began in a cozy kitchen with a family recipe book and a dream. After years of perfecting her pie recipes, Bethany decided to share her love for baking with the community. Our store has grown from a humble start to a beloved local gem, known for its warm atmosphere and irresistible pies. Each pie is handcrafted with the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, reflecting Bethany's dedication to quality and flavor. From the classic apple pie that started it all to new, inventive seasonal flavors, we strive to make every visit a delightful experience. Join us at Bethany's Pie Store, where every pie tells a story and every bite feels like home. We can't wait to share our passion for pies with you!
